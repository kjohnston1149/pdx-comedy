Are you a comic looking to get booked?

Most hosts/producers can be contacted through their Facebook Page, personal website, or even the event's Facebook page.

If you do reach out to a host, be sure to include a video of you performing- it's the best way for a host to decide how you would work on the show.

Also be sure to include important stuff like your name, any credits, and the date(s) of the show you're asking about.
